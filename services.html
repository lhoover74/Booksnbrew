FAQ

Questions about working together.

How much does a website cost?

Packages start at $125 for a clean starter site and go up to $600 for a premium multi-page build. Most hairstylists, barbers, and small businesses land in the $125–$300 range. Every project is custom, so final pricing depends on pages, features, and any add-ons — but you'll see the full cost before anything begins.

Do I have to pay everything upfront?

No. All packages are split into a deposit to start and a final payment after you approve the completed site. For example, the Starter Website is $60 upfront and $65 at the end. You never pay everything at once.

How long does the process take?

Most projects are completed within 1–3 weeks from kickoff. Timeline depends on scope and how quickly content and feedback come in. Rush delivery is available for an additional $75.

Do you build sites for hair stylists, barbers, and beauty brands?

Yes — this is one of the most common project types. We know what those businesses need: a clean professional look, a mobile-first layout, a booking or contact form, and a site that makes new clients feel comfortable reaching out. See our demos for examples.

What if I need more than what's in a package?

Add-ons are available for any package — extra pages, rush delivery, booking systems, e-commerce, and more. If your project needs something custom, fill out the quote form and we'll figure out the right approach together.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch?

Yes. Monthly maintenance plans start at $25/month and include updates, minor content changes, and general site upkeep so your site stays current and running smoothly.

Can you redesign my current website?

Absolutely. Redesigns are available as a project type. Depending on your current setup, we can update what's there or start fresh with a completely new build.
